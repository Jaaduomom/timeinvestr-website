Data Safety

How TimeInvestr handles your data for Play Store compliance

Data Collection & Usage

TimeInvestr collects and uses data solely for app functionality and user experience improvement. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Data We Collect

  • User Account Data: Email address, display name, and profile information for account management and app personalization
  • App Usage Data: Time tracking entries, goal settings, productivity metrics, and app interaction patterns
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, and app version for compatibility and performance optimization
  • Analytics Data: Anonymous usage statistics to improve app features and user experience

How We Use Your Data

  • Core Functionality: Time tracking, goal management, productivity insights, and app features
  • Personalization: Customize your experience based on your usage patterns and preferences
  • Performance Optimization: Improve app performance, fix bugs, and enhance user experience
  • Support Services: Provide customer support and troubleshoot technical issues

Data Security

  • Encryption: All data is encrypted in transit and at rest using industry-standard protocols
  • Secure Storage: Data is stored on secure, cloud-based servers with regular security audits
  • Access Controls: Strict access controls ensure only authorized personnel can access user data
  • Regular Updates: Security measures are regularly updated to protect against emerging threats

Data Retention

  • Account Data: Retained as long as your account is active
  • Usage Data: Retained for app functionality and deleted upon account deletion
  • Analytics Data: Aggregated and anonymized data may be retained for research purposes

Your Rights

  • Access: View and download your personal data
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Portability: Export your data in a machine-readable format

Third-Party Services

TimeInvestr uses the following third-party services for essential functionality:

  • Firebase: Authentication, cloud storage, and analytics (Google's privacy policy applies)
  • Google Play Services: In-app purchases and notifications (Google's privacy policy applies)

Notification Permissions

We use scheduled alarm permissions (SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM) to deliver precise productivity reminders and focus session notifications. This permission ensures notifications arrive at the scheduled time to help you maintain your productivity schedule and achieve your goals effectively.

Children's Privacy

TimeInvestr is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will take steps to delete such information. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so that we can take the necessary actions.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Data Safety policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes through the app or via email.

Contact Us

If you have questions about our data practices or would like to exercise your data rights, please contact us at:

  • Email: privacy@timeinvestr.com
  • Support: Contact Support
