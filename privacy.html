Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 9, 2025

Overview

TimeInvestr ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our productivity app.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Account Information: When you sign in with Google, we collect your email address and basic profile information
  • Usage Data: We collect data about how you use the app, including:
    • Task categories and time spent
    • Productivity metrics and goals
    • App settings and preferences

Local Storage

  • All your task data, goals, and statistics are stored locally on your device
  • We use SharedPreferences to store your app data securely

Cloud Storage (Optional)

  • If you choose to sign in, your data is synced to Firebase Cloud Firestore
  • You can disable cloud sync at any time in settings
  • Cloud data is automatically deleted when you sign out

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the app's functionality
  • To sync your data across devices (if enabled)
  • To improve app performance and user experience
  • To send notifications about your goals and tasks

Data Security

  • All data is encrypted in transit and at rest
  • We use industry-standard security practices
  • Your data is never sold to third parties

Your Rights

  • Access: You can view all your data within the app
  • Delete: You can delete your account and all associated data
  • Export: You can export your data through the app settings
  • Opt-out: You can disable cloud sync at any time

Third-Party Services

  • Google Sign-In: For authentication (Google's Privacy Policy applies)
  • Firebase: For cloud storage (Google's Privacy Policy applies)
  • Firebase Analytics: For app usage analytics (anonymized data only)
  • Google Play Billing: For in-app purchases and donations (Google's Privacy Policy applies)
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging: For push notifications (Google's Privacy Policy applies)

Notification Permissions

We use scheduled alarm permissions (SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM) to deliver precise productivity reminders and focus session notifications. This permission ensures notifications arrive at the scheduled time to help you maintain your productivity schedule and achieve your goals effectively.

In-App Purchases and Donations

TimeInvestr offers optional in-app purchases and donation features:

  • Time Coins: Virtual currency for in-app features (consumable purchases)
  • Donations: Optional support for app development (non-consumable purchases)
  • Payment Processing: All payments are processed securely through Google Play Billing
  • Purchase Data: We only receive confirmation of successful purchases, not payment details
  • Refunds: Refund requests are handled through Google Play Store

Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will take steps to delete such information. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so that we can take the necessary actions.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the app.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

  • Email: privacy@timeinvestr.app
  • Website: https://timeinvestr.com

Data Retention

  • Local data: Stored until you uninstall the app
  • Cloud data: Stored until you delete your account or disable sync
  • Analytics data: Retained for up to 26 months (anonymized)

International Users

If you are using our app from outside your country of residence, please note that your information may be transferred to and processed in countries with different privacy laws.

This Privacy Policy is effective as of January 2025 and will remain in effect except with respect to any changes in its provisions in the future.
