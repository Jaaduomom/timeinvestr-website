Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about TimeInvestr

How does TimeInvestr work?

TimeInvestr helps you track your daily activities and convert time into monetary value. You set your hourly rate, log your tasks, and see how your time investments grow over time.

Is my data secure?

Yes! All your data is encrypted and stored securely. We never sell your information to third parties, and you have full control over your data.

Can I sync across multiple devices?

Absolutely! Sign in with Google to enable cloud sync and access your data from any device. You can also disable sync anytime in settings.

Is the app really free?

Yes! TimeInvestr is 100% free with no ads and no premium features. All functionality is included at no cost.

How do I get started?

Download the app, sign in with Google, set your hourly rate, and start tracking your time! The app will guide you through the setup process.

What if I find a bug?

Please report any bugs to support@timeinvestr.app. Include your device type, app version, and a description of the issue for faster resolution.
