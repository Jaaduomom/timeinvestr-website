Data Deletion Guide

How to delete your data from TimeInvestr

Last updated: January 2025

Your Data, Your Control

At TimeInvestr, we believe in complete user control over personal data. You can delete all your data directly from within the app at any time. This includes all your time tracking data, goals, settings, and any cloud-synced information.

Account Deletion

Google Sign-In Integration

Since TimeInvestr uses Google Sign-In, you don't have a separate account with us. To delete your data and stop using the app completely:

  1. Open TimeInvestr app
  2. Go to Settings
  3. Tap "Sign Out"
  4. Your data will be automatically deleted from our servers

Note: Signing out will permanently delete all your data from our servers. If you want to keep your data but stop syncing, use the "Delete All Data" option instead.

How to Delete Your Data

1

Open TimeInvestr App

Launch the TimeInvestr app on your device

2

Go to Settings

Tap on the Settings icon in the bottom navigation bar

3

Scroll to Data Management

Scroll down to find the "Data Management" section in the settings

4

Select "Delete All Data"

Tap on "Delete All Data" option in the Data Management section

5

Confirm Deletion

Read the confirmation dialog and tap "Delete" to permanently remove all your data

Visual Guide

Data Management section in TimeInvestr Settings

What Gets Deleted

Time Tracking Data

All your daily time logs, task entries, and productivity history

Analytics & Statistics

All charts, reports, and statistical data about your productivity

Goals & Subgoals

All your set goals, subgoals, and progress tracking data

Settings & Preferences

All app settings, custom categories, and personal preferences

Cloud Data

All data synced to cloud storage (if you have cloud sync enabled)

Account Information

All account-related data and authentication information

Important Notice

Data deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. Once you delete your data, all your time tracking history, goals, and settings will be permanently removed from both your device and any cloud storage. Make sure you have backed up any important data before proceeding.

Alternative Options

Export Data First

Before deleting, you can export your data from the app settings to keep a backup

Pause Instead of Delete

Consider pausing data collection instead of deleting if you might want to return later

Reset Settings

You can reset specific settings without deleting all your data

Need Help?

If you're having trouble finding the data deletion option or have questions about the process, please contact our support team.
